ARTURIA – KEYLAB 88 (By Mark)

The behavior of all knobs and switches is configured in Bitwig under the controller settings.
Only hard-coded behaviors are shown below.
GLOBAL
Volume Master track volume
Sound / Multi Selects Arrange/Mix/Edit mode.

Arrange Mode: Press Sound.
Mix Mode: Press Multi.
Edit Mode: Press Sound+Multi
Pressing either button once in the selected mode will show/hide the devices panel.
F1-9 Volume for tracks 1-9
TRANSPORT
« Transport rewind
» Transport fast forward
Stop Stop or Tap Tempo (when stopped)
Play Transport play
Rec Transport record
Loop Transport toggle loop
